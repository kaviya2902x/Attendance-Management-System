About On-Duty Requests

On-duty requests are for official work assignments outside the regular workplace. These requests require supervisor approval and will be counted as working hours.

Temporary Solution

For now, you can use the following workaround for on-duty requests:

  1. Submit a regularization request for the days you were on duty
  2. In the reason field, mention "On-duty assignment"
  3. Attach any supporting documents via email to your supervisor
  4. Your supervisor will process it as a special case
Note: This is a temporary solution until the on-duty module is released.
